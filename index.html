About Me

Bridging Medicine and Design

I am a dedicated medical student at Jimma University with a passion for both healthcare and creative design. My unique background allows me to approach design problems with analytical thinking while maintaining artistic creativity.

With several years of experience in graphic design, I've worked on various projects ranging from medical illustrations to branding and marketing materials. I believe that good design can enhance communication in healthcare and make complex medical information more accessible.
